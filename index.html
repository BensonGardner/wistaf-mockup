How Wisconsin invests in civil justice for all.

Prime Partner Spotlight

A Prime Partner since 2021, CIBC Bank goes above and beyond IOLTA participation requirements by paying additional interest on its clients' IOLTA accounts â€” helping to expand access to civil legal services in Wisconsin. CIBC Bank's participation reflects an admirable commitment to justice for all in Wisconsin.

Since 1986, the Wisconsin Trust Account Foundation (WisTAF) has aided in "the administration of justice" by overseeing funds and investing in services and projects, including over $61.8 million in grants to increase people's access to justice in matters of civil law.

The Wisconsin Supreme Court established WisTAF because the Constitution doesn't guarantee an attorney in civil cases, yet when people don't have attorneys, a hole is created in the hull of our justice system. Not only are clients' own livelihood and safety at stake, but all of us lose through inefficient court operations and the need for increased public support and emergency services.

Justice shouldn't depend on how much money you make. Help us make this a reality.
